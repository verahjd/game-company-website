About Us

KapiKum Games is an independent game development studio proudly based in Baguio City, Philippines. We specialize in role-playing games that immerse players in richly crafted worlds with meaningful choices. Our team blends local talent, cultural inspiration, and modern dev practices to deliver experiences that feel fresh and globally competitive.
